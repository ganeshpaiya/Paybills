1. What is RINcash wallet? Why should I have it?


RINcash wallet is a secure online wallet where you receive cashback points through promotional offers of RechargeItNow which you can use for Recharges, Bill Payments and other services at RechargeItNow.

2. How can I make RINcash account?


The RINcash wallet can be activated in following ways.

Promotional Campaign: When you participate in given promotional campaign of RechargeItNow, you will receive sms & email with instructions to activate the same.
You can also create / activate the wallet by following given steps.

  • Desktop Site: Go to RINcash wallet on top right side header>Login>Activate wallet.
  • Mobile Site: Login>My Account>RINcash wallet>Activate wallet.
  • Mobile App: Select RINcash Wallet from Menu>Activate wallet.

12. Is there forfeiture of my wallet balance?


RINcash points expire one month after the end of calendar month in which these points were received unless utilized before expiry date. Example:

  • If you received cash back 3 times in the month of January 2017 (100 points on Jan 1, 100 points on Jan 15 & 100 points on Jan 31) and do not redeemed any points till Feb 2017 then all 300 points will expire on 1st March 2017.
  • If you received cash back 3 times in the month of January 2017 (100 points on Jan 1, 100 points on Jan 15 & 100 points on Jan 31) and you have consumed 150 points in Feb 2017 then balance 150 points will expire on 1st March 2017.
